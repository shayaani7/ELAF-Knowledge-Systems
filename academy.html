Telecom Middle East & Europe

Islamic Insurance (Takaful) Model for a Multinational Telecom Provider

Impact

3 GCC countries · 40,000+ policyholders

The Challenge

A multinational telecom corporation with operations across the GCC and Europe required an embedded insurance product for device protection that could be offered to Muslim customers without violating Gharar (uncertainty) prohibitions.

Our Solution

Structured a Wakalah-model Takaful product specifically engineered for device protection, including contribution calculation, surplus distribution methodology, and reinsurance (Retakaful) advisory.
